About Project

Project Overview: Adventure Works Cycles, a renowned international bicycle manufacturer, offers an exclusive 20% employee discount on various product lines. To streamline this program and enhance employee experience, we have developed a comprehensive PowerApps solution. This application serves as a "shop front," allowing staff members to easily browse, purchase, and manage their discounted products.

Features: Here are the high priority features successfully integrated into the solution:

  • Product Showcase: Employees can effortlessly view Adventure Works' product catalogue, sorted by category and price. Browsing through the extensive offerings has never been this user-friendly.
  • Manager Notifications: Line managers are notified via email as soon as a direct report creates an order, always keeping them in the loop.
  • Manager Approvals: Line managers can approve orders swiftly, whether via email or the app itself, ensuring a seamless workflow.
  • Discounted Delight: The Application displays the discounted prices for products, making it easy for employees to see the list price and the price after a 20% discount â€“ a true shopping delight!
  • Cart Management: It's now very easy to update, add, and remove items from the shopping basket. The first goal is providing a flawless purchasing experience.
  • Swift Checkout: The checkout process is a snap, allowing employees to create orders with ease and efficiency.
  • Dashboard Insights: For line managers, a comprehensive dashboard provides insights into direct reports' purchases.
  • Quick Search: Looking for a specific product by code or name? The PowerApp's search feature ensures employees find what they need without any hassle.
  • Order Confirmation: After submitting an order, employees receive immediate details, ensuring transparency and eliminating any room for confusion.

    • Conclusion: The PowerApps solution for Adventure Works' employee discount program has revolutionized the way employees interact with their exclusive shopping benefits. By integrating high-priority features such as product showcase, discounted pricing, efficient cart management, and robust manager oversight, the application ensures a smooth, transparent, and efficient purchasing process. Additionally, the administrator features enhance data management, making it easier to keep the system updated.

    Adventure Works is now better equipped to excel in today's fast-paced market, providing a superior employee experience and maintaining efficient operational processes. This journey of building the PowerApp has been incredibly rewarding, marking a significant step forward in leveraging technology to drive business success.
